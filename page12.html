PARIS MATCH

Design a publication in which you give your personal view on one or more aspects of Paris.

Interpretation:

A tribute to the ‘Verlan’: a slang born in the slums of Paris. With the carve effect I want to glorify and immortalize a language that’s considered as rather negative and uncivilized in the traditional society.
