Design a record cover for one of the listed records. Try to image that the artist rejects the existing cover pro- posal and he has selected you to create a brand new concept. Also the labels, inside cover and/or the insert of the record are part of the assignmant.

Interpretation:
‘Songs for a dead pilot’: the dull colours and the cheap cardboard refer to the melancholic, slow, sad, atmosphere of the album. The cover consists of parachutes and wires which directly refer to the songs: ‘Born by the wires’ and ‘Condescent’.
The inside case represents a blueprint which is a metaphor for the complexity and technicity of an airplane. Both inside and outside cases are nished with a metal silver edge.
